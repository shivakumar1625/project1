  • Contact Us
  • Inquiry Phase: Buyer sends product inquiry. Company responds with product details, specifications, and pricing.
  • Quotation Phase: Buyer requests a formal quote. Company provides a proforma invoice with price, terms, and shipping details.
  • Order Confirmation: Buyer reviews and confirms the order. Company sends order confirmation and timeline.
  • Production/Processing Phase: Company starts manufacturing or preparing the goods. Quality control checks at different stages of production.
  • Payment Processing: Buyer processes payment as per agreed terms (advance or Letter of Credit). Company verifies payment.
  • Packaging & Documentation: Company packages products securely for international shipment and prepares export documentation.
  • Shipping/Logistics: Goods are handed over to the shipping agent. Company provides tracking and shipping details to the buyer.
  • Customs Clearance: Products undergo export customs clearance. Buyer prepares for import customs clearance.
  • Delivery: Goods arrive at the destination port.
